  • Shawn William Feltmate

    "I am the proud Uncle of Royce Louis Dello Iacono, second born of all my nieces and nephews. I love my deceased nephew and will never forget how beautiful a spirit he presented in the physical world. Royce was a powerful soul that was ready to change the world with his positive energy and insight. He was always authentic when he met new people; which drew them in, because he didn't have a guard up like most people do in this life. It hurts me that he is not here anymore. But, I know where he went, and I am so happy for him. His whole life he wanted a father, and now, he is with THE FATHER."
