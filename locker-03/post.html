From the Heart of Aunt Barbara

  • Barbara Feltmate
  • 31 March 2023

For my never to be forgotten Nephew / Godson Royce, thank you for all those wonderful smiles and that charm you possessed. Thank you for letting me see the love you carried. You were one of a kind and I was blessed to have, had the honor to fight the fight with you. I learned so much in such a short time! Thank you for the new look at my own life and my life chooses. Life truly is unpredictable and precious. I thank you, it was an honor. Forever grateful to have known you Aunt Bobbie, and only you called me that, and I sure miss that. I love you beautiful Royce. Always missed. xoxoxo

Aunt Heather Remembers

  • Heather Gavranovic
  • 30 May 2023

Dear Roycester, You are surely missed. I am grateful to have had you in my life. Your spirit had an energy that is not seen often. Your ability to light up a room was uncanny. I loved talking with you about your future and your day to day life. I am grateful to have had the opportunity to be on the other end of the phone when you needed advice. When you were little you were a spitfire. A boy with lots of energy, joy, and curiosity for days. One of my favorite memories is when you came to Georgia as a christmas gift for Jeff. We put a big bow on your head. I loved out time together traveling to the Grand Canyon and beyond, then back again in a broken down RV. I love you Royce and know I will see you again one day.

Letter of Reflection from Aahme

  • Kristine Kramer
  • 9 June 2023

Dear Royce It has been very hard to start this letter because every time I start it I cry. I cry because I can't get one of your great bear hugs. I cry because your life on earth was so short. You were a wonderful young man, smart and very caring. I wish that I had more time with you. I wish that I could have been able to do more for you. I pray for your soul every night. I know you have earned the right to be with our Lord in heaven. I will never forget your wonderful smile. I wonder where your life would have taken you. Would you have married and had children of your own. (I'll bet you would have been a great father.) Royce you are and always will be in my heart. I will never forget my beautiful grandson. I thank our Lord that I was blessed to have you in my life. I love you so very much. Aahme
